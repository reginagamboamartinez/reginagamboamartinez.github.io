gracie abrams



        Gracie Marie Abrams is an american singer and songwriter ; her debut extended play "minor" was released on july 14 2020, her second EP " this is was it feels like " was released on november 12 , 2021 , incluiding singles "rockland" and "camden".

        Gracie was born on September 7 , 1999 in Los Angeles , California , she became interested in music from a young age , practicing different instruments , and after graduating high school , she studied international relations , but took a break to persue music.
        Before her releases , she started posting covers on youtube as well as her original songs , growing a following through social media , and her fan base and family connections led the industry to be attracted to her first songs . In october 2019 she relased her sebut single "mean it", to then on 2020 release her first EP "minor" followed by the second one in 2021 " this is what it feels like".
